## The Research Approach: Rigorous Science Meets Radical Exploration

1142 LABS operates at the intersection of evidence-based medicine and consciousness exploration. We are scientists and psychonauts. We value peer-reviewed research and personal experimentation. We believe in both the double-blind placebo-controlled trial and the N-of-1 case study.

### Our Methodology

1. **Rigorous Documentation** - Every claim is sourced. Every hypothesis is tested. Every result is recorded. We don't traffic in pseudoscience or wishful thinking. If something works, we document why. If something fails, we document that too.

2. **Consciousness Exploration** - We embrace psychedelic-assisted therapy, meditation, neurostimulation, and other consciousness-expanding modalities. The subjective experience of altered states provides insights that conventional neuroscience misses.

3. **Neurostimulant Optimization** - ADHD medications are not one-size-fits-all. We explore dosing strategies, combination therapies, and pharmacokinetic optimization to maximize cognitive benefits while minimizing side effects.

4. **Harm Reduction** - We acknowledge that people will experiment with their neurology—with or without medical supervision. Our role is to provide accurate information to minimize harm and maximize benefit.

5. **Neurodiversity Affirmation** - We reject the medical model that treats ADHD, autism, and other neurodevelopmental differences as deficits. These are variations in human neurology—different operating systems, not broken ones.

### Why This Is Controversial

Our approach challenges medical orthodoxy. We discuss off-label uses of medications. We explore psychedelic therapy before it's FDA-approved. We document substance combinations that no IRB would ever approve.

**But we do it transparently, rigorously, and with informed consent as the foundation.**

We don't advocate recklessness. We advocate informed autonomy. The right to explore one's own consciousness, to optimize one's own cognition, to make decisions about one's own neurology—with full access to the information needed to do so safely.
